About the Show

A space for the conversations that matter.

A State of Mind is a long-form interview podcast hosted by Julian Royce. Each episode features in-depth conversations with therapists, meditation teachers, researchers, and others doing meaningful work at the intersection of psychology, spirituality, and human flourishing.

The conversations are unhurried, curious, and aimed at depth rather than soundbites. No clickbait. No quick takes. Just thoughtful people exploring difficult questions together.

"What does it mean to live well? To heal? To wake up? To love and be loved? These are the questions worth our attention."
