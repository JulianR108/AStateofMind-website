The first session is also called an Intake Session. Julian Royce has developed a unique Intake Process that covers 4 fundamental pillars of Health and Wellbeing:

  • Sleep
  • Exercise
  • Diet & Nutrition
  • Social Relationships — including family, friendships, and communities you are a part of

We will also go over your relevant health history, your past experiences with therapy, coaching, and other related healing modalities — and look at what worked for you and what did not work so well for you.

We will go over your goals and intentions for your own growth and healing process. Finally, we will make a plan to move forward to best support you in your unique journey.

Julian is trained in multiple therapy and healing modalities. He currently offers:

  • Somatic (body-based) Therapy
  • Trauma Therapy
  • Couple's Counseling
  • Psychedelic-Assisted Therapy
  • Transformational Coaching
  • Medicine Ceremonies
