What to Expect After You Reach Out

  1. You'll book a free 20-minute consultation through Calendly, or send me an email or voicemail.
  2. If you booked through Calendly, you'll get an automatic confirmation. If you reached out by phone or email, I'll get back to you within one business day to set something up.
  3. We'll have our consultation. I'll listen, I'll answer your questions, and we'll see how it feels.
  4. If we both feel it's a good fit, we'll schedule a first full session.

If You're In Crisis

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please don't wait. Call or text 988 for the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, go to your nearest emergency room, or call 911. I am not able to provide emergency or crisis services.
